About Us

Borlaug Lifescience commenced its journey with a strong foundation in Agri-inputs, tailoring solutions to the unique challenges of African farmers. Established in 2019, our company has rapidly risen to prominence, driven by the profound expertise of our founding member, whose extensive experience in the African Agri-inputs industry spans over 20 years. Building on this expertise, we are strategically expanding our horizons to cater to a global audience.

The heart of Borlaug Lifescience draws inspiration from decades of navigating the most complex markets to ensure that all our business partners & farmers are equipped to overcome the challenges of food insecurity today and tomorrow in the African continent.
