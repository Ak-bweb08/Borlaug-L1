Services

At Borlaug Lifescience, we source, trade and formulate all kinds of agricultural inputs, speciality chemicals, public health products and equipment’s for agricultural use.

Empower your business with our comprehensive end-to-end services featuring our exclusive brands or our OEM solutions tailored for distribution as per our customers’ requirements. Streamline your operations with our Sourcing expertise, securing top-quality finished products at optimal prices. Expand globally with our Distribution services, offering worldwide supply and tailored product sourcing to meet your market demands. Unleash your potential with a partner dedicated to your success.

(A) Branded Approach

1. Our Brands’ Distribution

Borlaug Lifescience boasts a diverse portfolio of 100+ registered brands, reshaping distribution dynamics in Africa. Our branding strategy is a pledge to excellence, innovation, and meeting evolving industry needs. Each brand addresses specific challenges in crop protection, post-harvest management, and OEM equipment. We not only deliver quality products but also invite partners to join us in cultivating a sustainable and prosperous global agriculture future providing cutting-edge solutions to enhance agricultural offerings in a dynamic market.

2. Pricing

We strive to offer special prices to our registered branded customers to foster long-term partnerships, where their satisfaction is our priority. We prioritize a win-win pricing strategy, ensuring fair and flexible pricing models tailored to your product requirements.

3. Farmer Trainings

Borlaug Lifescience pioneers’ innovation in African agriculture, not only developing best practices but actively disseminating them for the welfare of farmers and the community. Periodically, we offer hands-on training services to our authorised distributors in modern scientific agricultural practices by empowering African growers for a sustainable future.

4. Promotional Material

Borlaug Lifescience provides promotional materials at a subsidized cost to its registered distributors to assist them in promoting our brands.

(B) OEM Approach

Our team are agile, adaptive, and engages in rigorous quality checks, ethical considerations, and a keen focus on environmental impact. We excel in developing and designing formulations, packaging, and equipment’s customized to fit your brand and market requirements by integrating ethical considerations, sustainability, and adaptability into our sourcing practices. Our versatility allows us to engage in transactions and deliver goods in any packaging of your preference. Whether bulk shipments, custom packaging, or specialized formats. We strive to uphold the values that define our commitment to excellence, customer and farmer satisfaction. Our dedication to excellence, innovation, and meeting industry demands knows no borders. Count on Borlaug Lifescience for the efficient and reliable worldwide delivery of agricultural solutions that meet your needs

Finished Goods :

Represent the pinnacle of convenience, providing customers with ready-to-use solutions for enhanced productivity. From precision-formulated solutions finished goods are meticulously crafted to deliver immediate benefits, ensuring optimal yields and sustainable farming practices.

Raw Materials / Specialty Chemicals :

Is designed to empower customers with customization options. Sourced with high-quality ingredients serves as the foundation for tailored formulations. Whether you're seeking specific raw materials or specialty chemicals, our solutions offer versatility and reliability, allowing you to fine-tune your usage methods according to your unique requirements.
